About Flood the Zone

Accountability after the headlines fade

"Flooding the zone" is a deliberate media strategy: release so many stories, so fast, that no single narrative can be held accountable. The public gets overwhelmed. Stories fade before they resolve. Nothing sticks.

Flood the Zone uses AI to do what no human newsroom can: track every major political story simultaneously, across hundreds of sources, over months and years — and report back on what actually happened.

We are not a news outlet. We are the accountability layer on top of the news.
